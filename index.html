About Infinite Wars

"Infinite Wars" invites you to immerse yourself in a casual yet thrilling play-to-earn (P2E) experience available on the Telegram platform. Assemble your team from an exciting roster of playable characters including the valiant Black Panther, the mystic Dr. Strange, the ingenious Iron Man, the agile Spiderman, the skilled Black Widow, the powerful Captain Marvel, and the fierce Gamora.

Initiating a game is seamless; a simple /startgame command springs you into action. Engage with the VnH GameMaster in private chat to select and command your hero in a strategic battle that unfolds over eight intense rounds, or until the forces of evil are vanquished.

Conceptualization and Design

Finalizing game concept and core mechanics. Designing the initial set of playable and NPC characters.

Development and Testing

Programming the basic game infrastructure. Developing the Telegram bot integration for gameplay. Alpha testing with a closed group to refine gameplay mechanics.

Pre-Launch Preparations

Introducing the game to potential players through marketing campaigns. Opening pre-registrations with exclusive early-bird rewards. Finalizing the in-game economy and P2E elements.

Launch

Officially launching "Infinite Wars" on Telegram. Releasing the GameMaster bot for public interaction. Hosting launch events with special rewards for early adopters.

Expansion and Growth

Rolling out new characters and villains to expand the universe. Implementing additional features such as guilds, alliances, and co-op missions. Introducing seasonal events and themed challenges.

Sustaining and Community Building

Regular updates with new content, balancing changes, and feature additions. Establishing a community-driven governance model for major game decisions. Hosting tournaments and eSports events to bolster competitive play.

Rewards can range from in-game currency and special abilities to cosmetic upgrades for your characters. The more you play and the better your strategies, the more you can earn. Specific details about the P2E system are outlined in the game's official documentation.
